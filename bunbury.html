Bunbury Senior-Accessible Dining Guide

Mobility Focus: All recommended venues feature flat/step-free entry, wide layouts, and close proximity to parking or direct passenger drop-off zones to support travelers with walking difficulties.

1. Lunch Recommendation: Bunbury Farmers Market

Bunbury Farmers Market Cafe

Highly Accessible

Location: 2 Vittoria Rd, Glen Iris (Right off the highway as you enter Bunbury).

Accessibility: Entirely flat layout, exceptionally wide indoor aisles, climate-controlled, and massive ACROD accessible parking bays directly at the front doors.

Top Ready-to-Eat Menu Highlights:

  • Hot Carvery: Tender, slow-roasted meats (roast beef or pork) served with hot vegetables and gravy. Soft and easy to eat.
  • Market-Made Sushi & Poke Bowls: Light, ultra-fresh options prepared daily by on-site chefs.
  • Award-Winning Market Pies: Signature flaky pastry pies filled with slow-cooked steak, chicken, or vegetables. Kept hot and ready to go.
  • Fresh Paninis & Wraps: Large, vibrant sandwiches that staff can toast hot on the spot.
Driving & Parking Tip: Take the Vittoria Road exit off Forrest Highway. Park in the designated ACROD blue bays right in front of the main automatic sliding doors. The entry is completely flush with the tarmac.

2. Dinner Options: Premier Waterfront & Marina Views

Modern developments built on flat concrete boardwalks with direct car drop-off areas and beautiful harbor vistas.

Vat 2

Top Marina View Pick

Cuisine: Modern Australian focusing on premium steaks, local wines, and fresh seafood.

The View: Premier waterfront positioning at the outer marina precinct with massive glass windows and a stunning deck facing the Indian Ocean inlet.

Accessibility: Flat threshold entry and an open-plan seating arrangement that is highly comfortable for navigating with walking aids or wheelchairs.

Driving & Parking Tip: Located at 2 Jetty Road. Drive down Jetty Road towards the marina. There is a dedicated car park right next to the restaurant with designated accessible spaces. The pathway from the car park to the entrance is fully paved and flat.

Aristos Waterfront Bunbury

Great For Windy Evenings

Cuisine: Casual, top-tier Australian seafood dishes (grilled barramundi, fish & chips, platters).

The View: Positioned directly on the harbor boardwalk looking out over the calm waters of Koombana Bay.

Accessibility: Features a large, fully enclosed glass patio equipped with wind shutters. Your senior can sit right at the water's edge while staying fully sheltered from chilly ocean breezes.

Driving & Parking Tip: Located at 42 Bonnefoi Boulevard. Pull into the Bonnefoi Boulevard roundabout loop directly in front of the restaurant boardwalk to drop your senior off safely on flat concrete. Accessible parking bays are available in the public lot immediately adjacent.

Silos Bar & Restaurant

Heritage Marina Spot

Cuisine: Modern Australian showcasing local Southwest produce.

The View: Overlooks yachts at the Marlston Hill Marina, built directly into Bunbury's iconic historic wheat silos.

Accessibility: Wheelchair-accessible parking, completely flat step-free entrance, spacious seating, and compliant disabled restrooms on-site.

Location: 1 Holman St (Inside Bunbury Hotel Koombana Bay).

Driving & Parking Tip: Turn onto Holman Street from Casuarina Drive. Use the hotel's main flat driveway entrance. There are dedicated accessible hotel parking bays right near the restaurant doors, entirely bypassing the multi-story car park.

The Cray Seafood & Grill Bunbury

Accessible

Cuisine: Elite seafood platters, fresh local fish, and premium steaks.

Accessibility: Ground-level concrete boardwalk right against the harbor with zero steps to enter. Wide seating pathways and fully accessible toilets.

Driving & Parking Tip: Located at 42 Bonnefoi Boulevard. Use the same drop-off point as Aristos. Drive directly to the boardwalk lip for a flat, step-free passenger transfer before moving your car to the nearby council parking lots.

The Bayview Bar

Card Only

Cuisine: Wood-fired pizzas, hearty pasta dishes, and local seafood.

The View: Soaring floor-to-ceiling windows to watch harbor boats and evening water activities.

Accessibility: Spacious ground-floor layout with excellent spacing between tables and zero stairs. Note: Cash is not accepted here.

Driving & Parking Tip: Located at 15 Bonnefoi Boulevard. The entire precinct shares the same coastal boardwalk parking structure. Drop off your senior directly at the curb of the flat promenade path leading right into the bar entrance.

Luxe Waterfront

Accessible

Cuisine: Fresh local catch of the day, rich seafood chowder, and authentic Sri Lankan curries.

Accessibility: Wheelchair-friendly entry doors, flat threshold entry, wide gap seating layout, and accessible restroom facilities.

Driving & Parking Tip: Located at Marina Pier, Bonnefoi Boulevard. Easily reached via the flat, continuous waterfront promenade. Drop-off zones allow a short, step-free walk into the building.
