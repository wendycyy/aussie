Trip snapshot

The essentials before you book

Travellers

3 adults — you and your parents (both b. 1950). Relaxed pace, easy walks only, no hikes or steep stairs.

Route

Perth → Mandurah → Bunbury → Busselton → Dunsborough → Margaret River → Augusta → direct back to Perth. Outbound sectors kept to ≈1 hour; the return is one longer ~4.5 hr drive with stops, trading the Bunbury overnight for a Perth finale.

Perth finale

The last night (11 Aug) and full day (12 Aug) are now in Perth CBD instead of Bunbury — Kings Park, Elizabeth Quay, WA Museum Boola Bardip, and a sunset rooftop bar before the 18:25 flight.

Whale season

Augusta (Flinders Bay) is in-season through August — your reliable whale-watching base. Dunsborough's season is just starting, so its cruise is bookable but tentative — call ahead.

Aurora Australis

Cape Leeuwin (34.4°S) is one of WA's better mainland spots for a chance, but it needs a strong geomagnetic storm (Kp 7+) — a bonus, not a guarantee.

Before you go

Local tips, wet-weather backups & booking checklist

Local tips & traps

  • Regional WA restaurants keep limited hours — book dinner ahead, especially Sat–Mon nights in Augusta.
  • Whale operators offer a free return trip if none are sighted — ask when booking.
  • Caves Road is scenic but narrow through forest — allow extra time after dark, watch for kangaroos at dawn/dusk.
  • August is the wet-season shoulder — pack layers and expect cool evenings in Augusta.
  • Mobile reception is patchy between towns — download offline maps each morning.

Rainy day alternatives

  • Dunsborough/Margaret River — Chocolate Company, indoor cellar-door tastings at Vasse Felix or Leeuwin Estate.
  • Busselton — the Jetty Underwater Observatory and train are covered.
  • Augusta — Glenarty Road Farm Shop's indoor cafe (note: Wild Lot Distillery is closed Mondays, open Fri–Sun only in winter), Augusta Historical Museum, covered tastings at Blackwood Wines.
  • Bunbury — Bunbury Regional Art Gallery and the Koombana Bay cafes.
  • Perth — WA Museum Boola Bardip and the Art Gallery of WA are indoors and free/low-cost — an easy fallback for the final day.

Booking checklist

  • ✔ Car rental — confirmed (#B89843500, 5 Aug 10:00 – 12 Aug 16:00, Perth Airport).
  • ✔ Bunbury, Dunsborough, Augusta and Perth accommodation — all confirmed, see Confirmed Bookings above.
  • Confirm BBQ facilities with the Cape Villas 23 (Dunsborough) and Storm Bay View (Augusta) hosts — not itemised on either booking.
  • Augusta whale watching cruise for 9 Aug — confirm the no-sighting return policy.
  • Dunsborough cruise (optional, 7 Aug) — call ahead to confirm the season has started.
  • Busselton Jetty timed ticket for 6 Aug.
  • Leeuwin Estate restaurant lunch (8 Aug) — book ahead, it gets busy.
  • Yallingup Cheese Company — small tasting fee, open Wed–Sun, ask about cheese-making classes.
  • Perth rooftop bar table for sunset (11 Aug) — Songbird or Rooftop at QT, book ahead for peak sunset slots.
  • Dinner reservations for Dunsborough and Augusta — limited options, book ahead.
