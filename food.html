Bunbury Senior-Accessible Dining Guide

Mobility Focus: All recommended venues feature flat/step-free entry, wide aisles, and close proximity to parking or direct passenger drop-off zones to support travelers with walking difficulties.

1. Lunch Recommendation: Bunbury Farmers Market

Highly Accessible

Location: 2 Vittoria Rd, Glen Iris (Right off the highway as you enter Bunbury).

Accessibility: Entirely flat layout, exceptionally wide indoor aisles, climate-controlled, and massive accessible parking bays directly at the front doors.

Top Ready-to-Eat Menu Highlights:

  • Hot Carvery: Tender, slow-roasted meats (roast beef or pork) served with hot vegetables and gravy. Soft and easy to eat.
  • Market-Made Sushi & Poke Bowls: Light, ultra-fresh seafood options prepared daily by on-site chefs.
  • Award-Winning Market Pies: Signature flaky pastry pies filled with slow-cooked steak, chicken, or vegetables. Kept hot and ready to go.
  • Fresh Paninis & Wraps: Large, vibrant sandwiches that staff can toast hot on the spot.

2. Dinner Option A: Waterfront Restaurants

Enjoy peaceful, lit-up night harbor views comfortably from a warm, wind-sheltered indoor table or flat boardwalk.

The Cray Seafood & Grill Bunbury

Top Waterfront Pick

Cuisine: Premium local Australian seafood platters, fresh fish, and premium steaks.

Accessibility: Ground-level concrete boardwalk right against the harbor with zero steps to enter. Spacious indoor seating and fully compliant accessible restrooms.

Senior Benefit: Pull the car up to the flat boardwalk edge on Bonnefoi Boulevard to drop off your senior safely at the entrance before parking.

Luxe Waterfront

Accessible

Cuisine: Fresh local catch of the day, rich seafood chowder, and authentic Sri Lankan curries.

Accessibility: Wheelchair-friendly entry doors, flat threshold entry, wide gap seating layout, and accessible restroom facilities on-site.

3. Dinner Option B: Chinese Cuisine

Traditional menus located in the flat CBD city center, just a 4-minute drive from the water.

China City Garden Restaurant

Top Traditional Pick

Cuisine: Long-standing local staple serving classic favorites like honey king prawns and sizzling satay chicken.

Accessibility: Completely flat, wheelchair-accessible entrance and accessible seating layout. Easy to pull up and drop off passengers right at the front door.

Address: 47 Victoria St, Bunbury.

Happy Dim Sim House

Accessible

Cuisine: Traditional Chinese and Malaysian dishes, highly praised for fried rice and steamed dumplings.

Accessibility: Peaceful atmosphere with dedicated wheelchair-accessible parking, accessible restrooms, and flat, spacious tables.

Address: 2/10 Ommanney St, Bunbury.

Green Jade

Accessible

Cuisine: Highly consistent Chinese dishes with generous portion sizes.

Accessibility: Located inside the modern Parks Centre shopping complex, featuring step-free entry, accessible parking bays, and shopping center disabled toilets.

Address: Parks Centre, Shop 22 Brittain Rd, Bunbury.

4. Dinner Option C: The Hybrid Choice (Waterfront + Asian)

Flow 36

Waterfront Check Evening Hours

Cuisine: Asian Fusion combining Malaysian Chinese, Vietnamese, and Thai flavors.

Accessibility: Sits on a completely flat, step-free boardwalk precinct with wide pathways, wheelchair-accessible entry, and disabled restrooms.

Senior Benefit: Pull the car directly into the flat boardwalk loop on Holman Street to drop off your senior at the front door. Note: Famous for lunch; check evening dinner availability or call ahead to book for a 19:00 slot.
